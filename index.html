Editing is a catalyst.

I view every piece of writing as a tangible symbol of the relationship between writer and reader. As both an editor and writer, I act as a catalyst to not only make that relationship possible, but to make it as powerful and moving as it can possibly be. 

Regardless of whether a text aims to inform, transform, entertain, or inspire, upon encountering it the reader should feel that he or she alone is the intended recipient of the author's ideas. At the same time, I believe that the publishing process is a collaborative effort that allows an author's work to reach its full potential and connect with growing, diverse audiences.

If you share this philosophy, I'd love to work with you. If you would like to learn more about me or discuss a freelance project, you can contact me at kathleenrconley@gmail.com. 

You can also find me on LinkedIn, Goodreads, Last.fm, I ✔ Movies, and Letterboxd.
